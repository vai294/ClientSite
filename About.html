BUDT748 - INDUSTRY PRACTICUM
BUDT748 is the capstone course for MS Information System (IS) majors, where students apply their knowledge
to develop real-world IS solutions for business clients. Through a complete IS development process, students
design and implement functional prototypes that solve practical business challenges preparing them for
careers in technology and consulting.
